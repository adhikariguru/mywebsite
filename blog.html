Nepal's Political Crisis Triggers Financial Downturn

Date: 10th March, 2023

By: Manish Adhikari

Nepal is currently facing an unprecedented political crisis that has severely impacted the country's financial market. The ongoing political turmoil has caused a loss of confidence among investors, leading to a severe decline in the stock market and a bearish sentiment pervading the financial market. The Nepal Rastra Bank's measures to regulate inflation have further exacerbated the liquidity situation, creating a challenging environment for the banking and financial sector. The prevailing bearish sentiment has had ripple effects across several sectors, impacting the overall economy.
