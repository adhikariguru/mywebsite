A Love So True

Her smile is a work of art, exquisite and true,

A vision of beauty that captures my heart anew,

Her lips curve upwards in a perfect arc,

A smile that's so radiant, it lights up the dark.


Her laugh is elegant, a sound that's so pure,

Like a melody that's perfect, in rhythm and allure,

It lifts me up, fills me with pure delight,

A symphony of joy that banishes the night.


Her face, a vision of loveliness untold,

A sight to behold, that never grows old,

Her features, so pretty, are a work of art,

A masterpiece that captures my heart.


Her nose, so fine and perfectly defined,

A feature that complements her face, so kind,

Her eyes, so pretty, they shine bright and true,

A window to her soul, reflecting all that's pure.


Her speech is innocent, a voice so sweet,

A sound that's so gentle, it's like a treat,

Her words, so pure, full of love and grace,

A heart that's pure, with a radiant face.


All these things make her so special to me,

A love that's true, that's pure and free,

With her by my side, my heart is complete,

Forever in love, forever at peace.
